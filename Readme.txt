Create Tax-Calculator Application

Requirement - 
1. Taxes
	a. 10% Basic Tax is applicable on all goods except Books, Food, and Medicines.
	b. 5% Import Duty is applicable on all imported goods with no exceptions.
2. Receipt – Your billing application should generate a receipt which includes
	a. One line item per product with – quantity, name of item and price including tax
	b. Totals
		i. Total Tax
		ii. Total Cost of all items with taxes
	c. Tax is rounded up to the nearest 5 Paise
3. Assume currency as INR
